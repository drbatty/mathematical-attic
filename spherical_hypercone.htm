Spherical Hypercone Spirals

This is a spherical hypercone rotating in 4D. A hypercone is the analogue of a cone in 3D, with spherical cross-sections instead of circular ones. It has been drawn using a spiral parametrization via polar co-ordinates. Another way to think of the positive branch of a spherical hypercone is the geometric shape traced out by the wavefront of a sound wave over time. (The one in the picture is a mathematical cone, with two branches.)
