S⁵pirals

Projection of lines in the 5-torus via spherical polar parametrisation. Things are getting more and more bunched up, making the central circle and its fibres more and more pronounced, whilst the rest of the sphere becomes the dark side of the moon. The roundedness of the five-sphere is difficult to discern. It looks more like two krakens having a fight somewhere in a 6D ocean.
